Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 30, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains what information LICENA ("LICENA", "we", "us"), operated by Armen Lalian in Los Angeles, California, collects and how we use it when you use our website and study tools (the "Service").

1. Information we collect

  • Account information — your email address, your name, and your password (stored only as a secure hash, never in plain text).
  • Usage information — the courses and guides you add, your study progress, your interface and study language, and any support requests or messages you send us.
  • Device & technical information — a device identifier we store to secure your account and limit account sharing, plus standard technical data such as IP address and browser type.
  • Assistant messages — messages you send to the in-app assistant, which are processed to answer you.

2. How we use your information

  • To provide the Service, sign you in, and save your study progress.
  • To send service emails (email confirmation, password reset, and updates about your requests).
  • To respond to your support requests.
  • For security and to prevent account sharing and abuse (device limits).
  • To maintain and improve the Service.

3. Service providers we share data with

We use trusted third parties to run the Service. They process data only to provide their service to us:

  • Supabase — database, authentication, and storage of your account and study data.
  • Anthropic — powers the in-app assistant; messages you send to the assistant are processed there to generate replies.
  • Resend — delivery of our service emails.
  • GitHub Pages / Vercel — website hosting and domain/DNS.
  • Stripe — payment processing (only once paid features launch).

We do not sell your personal information.

4. Cookies and local storage

We use your browser's local storage for essential functions only — to keep you signed in, remember your language, and store your device identifier. We do not use third-party advertising cookies.

5. Data retention

We keep your information while your account is active. You can ask us to delete your account and associated data at any time (see Contact).

6. Your choices and rights

You may access, correct, or delete your personal information by contacting us. If you are a California resident, you may have additional rights under California law (such as the right to know and to delete) — contact us to exercise them, and we will not discriminate against you for doing so.

7. Children

The Service is intended for adults preparing for professional licensing exams and is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Security

We protect your data with HTTPS, hashed passwords, and database access controls. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, but we work to protect your information.

9. International users

The Service is operated from the United States and your information is processed there.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date above reflects the latest version.

11. Contact

Questions or requests: armenlalian@gmail.com.
