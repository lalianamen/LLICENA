Our story

Built by people who actually sat that exam.

We went through the CSLB process ourselves — the paperwork, the wait, the study prep. What we couldn't find was a practice tool that worked the way we study: in our own language, focused on what we kept getting wrong, formatted like the real exam.

So we built one. No fluff, no upsells, no guarantee of passing. Just the closest thing to exam conditions we could put together, available in English, Spanish, Russian, and Vietnamese.
